不同光质下生菜生长差异及机理分析

   高中科创项目

项目摘要 / Project Summary

本研究聚焦蓝光和红光对生菜生长的影响，以农科院速生生菜种子为材料，在特定温湿度条件下培育，待其生长至 30 天，分别用白光、蓝光和红光处理 20 天。实验测定生菜鲜重、光化学淬灭系数、叶绿素含量等多项指标。结果显示，蓝光下生菜较为挺拔、叶片硬，红光下茎叶长、质地软；红光显著增加鲜重和叶面积，蓝光鲜重略增、叶面积略减；蓝光促进叶绿素合成，红光则抑制；红光和蓝光处理均使光保护能力受损，蓝光对初级光能转化有促进作用，红光则存在一定损伤。本研究为优化农业生产、设施园艺发展及保障食品安全提供理论依据。

This study focuses on the effects of blue and red light on the growth of lettuce. Seeds of fast - growing lettuce from the Academy of Agricultural Sciences were used as materials and cultivated under specific temperature and humidity conditions. After 30 days of growth, the lettuce was treated with white light, blue light, and red light for 20 days respectively. Multiple indicators such as the fresh weight of lettuce, photochemical quenching coefficient, and chlorophyll content were measured in the experiment. The results show that lettuce under blue light is more upright with hard leaves, while under red light, the stems and leaves are long and relatively soft. Red light significantly increases the fresh weight and leaf area, while blue light slightly increases the fresh weight and slightly reduces the leaf area. Blue light promotes chlorophyll synthesis, while red light inhibits it. Both red and blue light treatments damage the photoprotection ability. Blue light promotes the primary light energy conversion, while red light causes certain damage. This study provides a theoretical basis for optimizing agricultural production, the development of protected horticulture, and ensuring food safety.
