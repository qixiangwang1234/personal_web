2025美国数学建模大赛——H奖

STAIRS: Staircase-based Temporal Analytic for Inferring Rich Stories

获奖摘要 / Award Summary

我们的团队在2025年美国数学建模大赛中荣获H奖（Honorable Mention）。该项目提出了一种基于楼梯磨损的时空分析方法，通过创新的数学模型和算法解决了复杂的问题。我们的解决方案在超过10000支参赛队伍中脱颖而出，展现了出色的团队协作和数学建模能力。

Our team won the Honorable Mention (H Award) in the 2025 Mathematical Contest in Modeling. The project proposed a staircase wear-based spatiotemporal analysis method that solved complex problems through innovative mathematical models and algorithms. Our solution stood out among over 10,000 participating teams, demonstrating excellent teamwork and mathematical modeling skills.
